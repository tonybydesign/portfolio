About

The designer behind the screen.

Antonio Bautista-Foti

I'm Antonio, a visual designer and creative strategist based in St. Louis, MO, specializing in high-impact digital content and brand identity for the gaming and esports industry.

For the last six years, I've had the privilege of working with global brands like Immortals, Riot Games, and numerous collegiate programs, building visual narratives that resonate with passionate communities. My work blends strategic thinking with bold, dynamic execution—whether it's building an entire visual system from scratch or designing a single, viral social graphic. I believe good design isn't just about aesthetics; it's about solving problems and driving tangible results.

Let's build something unforgettable.
